About

A scientist with mountains in her bones 🌿

I'm Dechen — an environmental scientist currently pursuing my M.Sc. at the University of Leeds. My work sits at the intersection of geospatial analysis, climate adaptation, and the everyday lives of people most affected by environmental change.

Before Leeds, I spent two years at NAXA in Kathmandu, working alongside disaster risk reduction teams to turn raw field data into maps, infographics, and stories that communities and decision-makers can actually use. I love untidy questions, careful data, and any excuse to be outside.
